Bot Command Guide

Public Commands

/trivia – Test your MLS knowledge!
/check_leaderboards – See top predictors for current matchday. (prediction league)
/total_leaderboards – View the full standings for all matchdays together. (prediction league)
/standings – Display official MLS 2025 standings.
/news – Display news from the MLS or MLS Bot updates.
/suggestion – Send a bug report or suggestion to the creator.
/donor – Donate and become a member to help this bot going!

Career Mode Commands (Public)

/career_start – Begin your soccer journey as a Free Agent!
/sign_contract – Sign with a MLS academy!
/train [skill] – Train pace, passing, shooting, or defense and earn XP.
/my_stats – View your career mode stats.
/heal – Recover from an injury.
/simulate_match – Play a simulated match and earn XP!
/offers – Get team offers based on your stats!
/retire – End your career with a Hall of Fame message.
/hall_of_fame – See the top 10 legendary players on the server.

KickGo Commands

/start – Start KickGo and get your starter + KickCoins.
/catch – Try to catch new players for your collection.
/profile – View your KickGo stats, wins/losses, and currency.
/battle – Challenge others in penalty shootouts for KickCoins.
/dex – Browse the full index of KickGo players.
/buy – Purchase shop items for gameplay boosts.
/inventory – View your owned items.
/use – Activate or apply an item (e.g. catch boosts).
/leaderboards – View top KickGo players by wins, coins, or catches.

Prediction League Reps Only

/predict – Submit your matchday predictions.
/check_predictions – Review your submitted picks.

MLS Community Staff Only

/add_results – Add final match results to the system.
/inactivity_report – Report or review inactive reps.
