Help & Commands

Version: RC v1.2.2

Developer: calebrlycan

Last Updated: May 13, 2025

Public Commands

  • /trivia – Test your MLS knowledge!
  • /check_leaderboards – See top predictors for current matchday.
  • /total_leaderboards – View the full-season standings.
  • /standings – Display official MLS 2025 standings.
  • /news – Get all MLS and bot-related news.
  • /suggestion – Submit a bug or idea to the developer.

Career Mode (Public)

  • /career_start – Begin your soccer journey.
  • /sign_contract – Sign with an MLS academy.
  • /train [skill] – Train and earn XP.
  • /my_stats – View your stats.
  • /heal – Recover from injury.
  • /simulate_match – Play a match and earn XP.
  • /offers – Sign with new teams based on stats.
  • /retire – End your career in style.
  • /hall_of_fame – View top 10 legendary players.

Prediction League (Reps Only)

  • /predict – Submit your matchday predictions.
  • /check_predictions – Review your picks.

Staff Only

  • /add_results – Submit final match results.
  • /inactivity_report – Track inactive reps.
