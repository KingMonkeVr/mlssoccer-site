Bot Documentation

Discover all available commands for the MLS Community Bot.

Public Commands

  • /trivia – Test your MLS knowledge!
  • /check_leaderboards – View top predictors this week.
  • /total_leaderboards – See season-wide standings.
  • /standings – Official MLS 2025 standings.
  • /news – Latest MLS + bot news.
  • /suggestion – Send ideas or bug reports.

Career Mode

  • /career_start – Begin your career.
  • /sign_contract – Join an academy.
  • /train [skill] – Level up your player.
  • /my_stats – See your stats.
  • /heal – Recover from injury.
  • /simulate_match – Play a match.
  • /offers – Get contract offers.
  • /retire – Retire your player.
  • /hall_of_fame – See legendary players.

Prediction League (Reps Only)

  • /predict – Submit your predictions.
  • /check_predictions – Review your picks.

Staff Only

  • /add_results – Input match results.
  • /inactivity_report – Track inactive reps.
